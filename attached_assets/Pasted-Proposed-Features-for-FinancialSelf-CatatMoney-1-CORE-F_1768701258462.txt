4. PRIORITY FEATURE: "Debt Destroyer" (Debt Tracker)
How it Works:

Display: Total Debt: Rp 3,500,000.

Action: Every time a "Debt Payment" expense is logged, the bar shrinks.

Reality Check: If a user logs a "New Monitor" expense while debt is still high, a cheeky pop-up appears: "Are you sure? You still owe 2 million, bro."

Why You Need It: Keeps your financial priorities sane and keeps you accountable.

5. ANALYTICS FEATURE: "Net Worth Reality Check"
Users often feel "broke" when their wallet is empty, even if their savings are growing elsewhere.

How it Works: The main dashboard shows more than just "Wallet Balance." It displays your TOTAL NET WORTH: (SeaBank Balance + Wallet Cash + Envelope Savings) - (Remaining Debt).

Why You Need It: To help you realize your assets are actually growing, even when your physical pocket money feels low.
